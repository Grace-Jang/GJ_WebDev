SPAGHETTI WITH CLAMS

Learn how to cook in 15 minutes!

s p a g h e t t i with c l a m s

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti pasta
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams
  • olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • ½ teaspoon minced garlic
  • black pepper
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Boil water and add salt for pasta
  2. add clams and olive oil
